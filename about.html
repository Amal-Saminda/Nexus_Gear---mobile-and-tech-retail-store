Our Mission

At Nexus Gear, our mission is to democratize technology access across Sri Lanka. We believe that everyone deserves access to the latest innovations, from cutting-edge smartphones to powerful laptops and essential accessories.

We're committed to bridging the digital divide by offering genuine products at competitive prices, backed by exceptional customer service and reliable support.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 as a small electronics store in Colombo, Nexus Gear has grown to become Sri Lanka's largest mobile and electronics retailer. Our journey began with a simple vision: to make technology accessible to everyone.

Today, we serve customers across the island with our comprehensive online platform, multiple retail locations, and island-wide delivery network.
